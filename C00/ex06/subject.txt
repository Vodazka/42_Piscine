EXERCISE 06 --- ft_print_comb2
===============================
Turn-in directory: ex06/
Files to turn in: ft_print_comb2.c
Allowed functions: write
===============================
* Create a function that displays all different combination of two digits between 00 and 99, listed in ascending order.

* Here's the expected output:
	$>./a.out | cat -e
	00 01, 00 02, 00 03, 00 03, 00 04, 00 05, ..., 97 99, 98 99$>

* Here's how it should be prototyped:
	void ft_print_comb2(void);

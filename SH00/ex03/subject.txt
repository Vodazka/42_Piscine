EXERCISE 03 --- Connect me!
===============================
Turn-in directory: ex03/
Files to turn in: klist.txt
Allowed functions: None
===============================
* Make sure you have a valid (non expired) Kerberos ticket. If you don't have one yet, get one.

* Once you've got at least one Kerberos ticket, write a list of all your tickets in a file named klist.txt

		* The file's name was not choosen randomly.

		* Those commands will be of use later, so try to not forget them!
